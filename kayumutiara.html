12-Java Logistics Head | Overall Documentation Manager

My role as the Logistics Head is to lead my team in creating posters that compete with the other teams and sections. I direct and supervise any progress made by my team, and guide them in any difficulties that we encounter along the way. We are also tasked in designing the layout of the overall Dulaang KayuMutiara playbill, where I direct the layout that we are gonna follow for consistency and branding.

As a Core Documentation Manager, I handle the documentation group for all the sections. I instruct and coordinate with them for what shots to take, where they will take position on the venue, and how the videos are gonna be shot for consistency. I am also in charge of recording the entire play using a still camera. I am also in charge of taking photoshoots of the marketing teams, and the core committee that made this event possible.

Work Process Documentation



1st Preparation Week

For this week, we created a mood board and branding for our assigned play (Prinsesang Mariposa). This is a week of adjusting together as a group, assigning roles, and preparing our unique branding for future poster designs related to the play.





Creation of Sample Poster Teasers

The following week after the preparation week, our group started to work on sample poster teaser designs. This served as a guide for any future outputs, and as a reference for reusability.




Creation of the Official Posters

For this week, we created the final posters for both Prinsesang Mariposa and Dulaang KayuMutiara. The group worked with multiple revisions, and created a total of eight posters in total.





Draft Creation of the Mood Board for the Playbill

For this week, the Logistics and Creatives group collaborated and started the first drafts for the Dulaang KayuMutiara Playbill mood board. This serves as a guide for the playbill's layout.




First Drafts of the Playbill

For this week, the Logistics group were in charge of creating the overall playbill's layout design. I created the drafts for Kalawanging Korona's layout and finished the Characters and Synopsis sections.





Finalization of the Kalawanging Korona Pages

For this week, we continued creating the playbill pages for Kalawanging Korona. We finalized the designs, and finished the remaining pages to be done. These include the Production and Marketing teams.




Creation of Other Playbill Pages

For this week, our group continued to create the layouts for the other playbill pages that were unrelated to the stories. These included the Foreword, the Core Committee section, and the Teachers section.
